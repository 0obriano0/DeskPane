# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the DeskPane community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Public or private abuse.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or deliberately derailing discussions.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers will review and investigate reports and will respond in a way that is appropriate and respectful to the situation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and any public communication that represents the project.
